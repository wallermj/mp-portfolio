WEEK 5

WEEK 5

Editing Audio

Video/Audio

Today I spent the day editing our audio interview. I learnt how to cut and edit the clips and then add into the Multiwave section and adding it all together. 

By editing the audio and learning how to do this has meant I've been able to finish the file for our assignment


Adding Social Icons

Web Design

Today we were demonstrated how to add social media icons to my home and contact pages. We did this using a website called font where you can search the icon you want, copy the html link and add it to atom. We also learnt how to change the colour of the icon; we did this by adding style= ”color #0000”.

By learning how to do this it has meant I was able to change the already existing icons that didn’t relate to the text, to relatable social icons. I changed the icons to Instagram, twitter and email. This was on the index page where I give information to contact me. Along with my social media channels.

Video/Audio

In today's 2-hour session we had a guest speaker. Our guest speaker was a football journalist called Kevin Palmer, he spoke about his career within football journalism, how he got to where he is 25 years later and all his tips and tricks. 

Even though I don’t want to go into journalism, it was very interesting to listen to our guest speaker and how he built job roles and his way up. It also gave a good insight into the media world in general.


Digital Wednesday

Today we went to film Bianca Walkden our interviewee. We were taught how to film prior to filming the interview, including the 180-degree angle shot, noddies, answering and asking the questions. 

Our edited video interview will go on the video page of my website.  


Embedding My Own Images Into My Website

Web Design

Today we learnt how to import my own images into my website. Additionally, me and my peers worked out how to link images to additional pages. 

By learning how to link images to additional pages this meant that on my design log page of my web site I was able to link a ‘Week 1’ page a ‘Week 2’ page etc. Furthermore, by being able to insert my own images onto page this has meant that I have completed my photography page and my index page.


Submission Day!

Web Design

Today is submission day so I have been finishing my design log, and embedding it into Atom. Additonally, I have been doing some final touch on all my pages.
