WEEK 4

Infographic

Web Design

Today Jennie talked to us about infographics and how we can include them within our website. She showed us a website called Canva which is where we chose a template for our infographic. I then changed the background colours and other colours to relate to the topic I spoke about. Jennie also spoke about how an infographic is also about story telling.  When creating an infographic is must be something people want to know about.

This session was helpful as I’m planning to include an infographic into my website. By using Canva this made it easier for me create an infographic. 

Video

Lawrence taught us about the basics of video production. He covered about framing, the rule of thirds and shot types. He then showed us the basics of editing on Adobe Premier Pro. He then split us into groups to film a short five sequence video so we could demonstrate the basics of video production.

This helped me develop my video production skills.

Importance of User Experience

Web Design

Today we spoke about the importance of user experience when it comes to designing a website. When producing a website you should always put yourself in the target audiences shoes and assess whether it was a good experience.

This is helpful for my assignment as it is important that my target audience get the best possible experience they can. 

Video

In today's session we were introduced to the filming cameras we will be using for the video interview in our assignment. We also learnt the basics to video; these were holding your shots, framing, focus, exposure, white balance and audio quality. We were taught how to put together the tripod and safely attach the camera. The Basics part of the tripod are the head, the legs, the bubble and the spreader. Additionally, we were introduced into the pan and tilt lock, you can get some great shots with this as it will make the shot look smooth.

By learning the video basics and everything that makes a good video interview, once I've practiced what we have learnt will help towards our group video interview assignment. By learning how to put the tripod safely together and locking it all, is good for ourselves and others health and safety. Also, by knowing what not to do also helps for when we film our video as we will be aware of what could go wrong and what we shouldn’t do. Intern this helps us with getting a good piece of work.

Digital Wednesday

For digital Wednesday we were asked to upload an audio recording of 30 seconds to teams. This was a personal update of how we were getting along with our portfolio website. 

This was good for me as it made me look back at my progress and also what I need to complete.

Embedding Audio

Web Design

Today we were showed how to embed the audio interview. The way we did this was via SoundCloud. On this website we were taught how to get the embedded link and copying it over to atom and uploaded on our website.

This was helpful as it made uploading my audio interview a lot easier.

Video

Today we filmed a news report using the 180-degree rule. This rule is important to getting a good interview. It suggests that two characters in a scene should maintain the same left/right relationship to one another. We all had a go at recording and listening to the audio on the interview. 

Actually, practicing how to use the equipment has helped develop skills of video and preparing for our group video interview assignment.
